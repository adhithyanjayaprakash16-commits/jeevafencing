About Us

Trusted Fencing Experts in Tamil Nadu

Jeeva Fencing Company is a locally-owned, family-run business based in Keela Pandarapuram, Ittamozhi, proudly serving Thisayanvilai and surrounding areas for over a decade.

We specialize in supplying and installing a wide range of fencing solutions — from agricultural fencing and compound walls to industrial perimeter security. Our mission is simple: deliver the highest quality fencing at the fairest price, with honest service every step of the way.

  • ✅ Fully licensed and insured company
  • ✅ Serving 50+ villages across Tirunelveli district
  • ✅ Both supply-only and full installation services
  • ✅ Custom fencing designs as per your requirement
